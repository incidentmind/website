# IncidentMind Launches Custom AI Automation Platform to Eliminate Manual Work Across Enterprise Engineering Teams

**Durham, NC, USA — March 19, 2026**

Today, IncidentMind LLC, a boutique AI platform engineering firm, announced the general availability of its Custom AI Automation Platform — a bespoke, production-grade service that designs and delivers AI-driven automation systems tailored to each client's unique infrastructure, workflows, and compliance requirements. Whether eliminating manual incident response, automating developer toil, building AI-powered data pipelines, or deploying Model Context Protocol (MCP) agents for internal tooling, IncidentMind builds the system the client actually needs — not a generic SaaS product.

Unlike off-the-shelf automation tools, every IncidentMind engagement begins with a deep technical assessment of the client's environment and ends with a working system running in the client's own infrastructure. Founder and engineer Viktor Burdyey commented, "Most vendors sell you a platform and leave you to figure out the implementation. We do the opposite — we start from your problem and build backward to the right solution. At Fidelity Charitable I built a self-improving AI incident platform with feedback loops and enterprise governance. Before that I scaled EAT24 from zero to a $134M Yelp acquisition. Every engagement brings that same hands-on depth."

Representative automation systems delivered by IncidentMind include AI-driven incident resolution platforms that cut mean-time-to-resolution by 60% and reduce alert noise by 40%; MCP server implementations that give AI agents safe, governed access to internal developer tooling; CI/CD and platform engineering automation that eliminate deployment bottlenecks; and custom LLM pipelines that process operational context at enterprise scale without sending sensitive data to third-party clouds. According to independent industry analysis, $250B is spent annually on manual IT operations globally, and 73% of engineering teams report that existing AI tooling doesn't integrate with their actual stack — a gap IncidentMind closes through direct implementation, not consulting decks.

IncidentMind LLC, founded by Viktor Burdyey, is a platform engineering and AI consultancy headquartered in Durham, NC, operating fully remote with enterprise clients. With over 20 years of hands-on engineering experience — including a $134M acquisition exit and production AI systems processing 12GB of aggregated operational context at Fidelity Charitable — IncidentMind brings the rare combination of startup velocity and enterprise-grade reliability to every engagement.
